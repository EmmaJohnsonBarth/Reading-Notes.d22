Welcome to the home page

Hello, my name is Emma and welcome to my page. Here you will learn a bit about me.

Biography

I was born in Kentucky, but moved with my family to Mount Pleasant, Iowa when I was 9. I've lived in Iowa ever since! I graduated from Mount Pleasant Community High School, where I was active in band, choir, and theatre as well as taking several AP classes.

Education

In 2013, I graduated from Cornell College in Mount Vernon, Iowa. I majored in Biochemistry and Molecular Biology and graduated with a 3.5 GPA. I remained in band and choir throughout choir, and was involved in a sorority, Beta Psi Eta.

Work Experience

After graduating college, I worked for about two years for Roquette America Inc, where my job was performing quality control testing on high fructose corn syrup and dextrose. After that, I was able to get a job with the University of Iowa Pharmaceuticals. In this role, I performed quality control testing as well as other studies on pharmaceutical drug products. Most of these were in various stages of clinical research. I was in this position for eight years before I changed fields to pursure computer science.

Interests
